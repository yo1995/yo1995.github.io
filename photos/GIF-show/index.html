This is the collection of all the GIF I’ve made during last two years.

Most of them are achieved with matlab, some of them COMSOL and Photoshop. They can be categorized into four major groups: nonlinear-physics, solid state physics, electro-magnetic wave and multiphysics. There are also one picture featuring my verilog VLC project.

If you’d like to get better knowledge of any of them, please comment below or contact me with email.

Through the semesters, I’ve been working on several different fields and got some basic knowledge of those subjects. Some topics which are too difficult for most of us to comprehend drive me to figure out better ways to exhibit the real essence beneath calculations and deductions.

For example, the brillouin region of Fcc might be a little bit difficult for the students to figure out since it’s a dodecahedron. With the help of matlab, I could draw both the static picture and the evolution of the energy band along with the change of wave vector.
